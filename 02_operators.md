1. Concept

Operators in C# are symbols used to perform operations on values and variables. They are used for calculations, comparisons, and decision making in a program.

Arithmetic operators like +, -, *, and / are used for math calculations. Comparison operators like ==, !=, >, and < are used to compare values and return true or false. Logical operators like && and || are used to combine multiple conditions.

Operators help control the logic of a program and determine what actions should be taken based on different conditions.
